## Typography

Handwriting stack (Segoe Print / Chalkboard SE) for everything; headings are bigger
handwriting with a rotated hand-drawn underline; mono only for code.

## Signature moves

- Bright yellow paper ground; all text in ink blue, never black; ±1–2° card rotation.
- Three card grammars mixed freely: pinned cream note, dashed yellow slip, kraft block
  with hard ink offset shadow.
- tabbed-gallery: pinned paper strips as tabs, active strip sits straight with a brass pin.

## Fit

Best for: workshops and brainstorm readouts, zine-flavored idea shares.
Avoid for: formal executive reviews, dense data tables.
