## Typography

Heavy (800) Helvetica/Arial for everything; uppercase display, tight tracking, tabular
figures.

## Signature moves

- Visible construction grid; 2px black borders; square corners; zero shadows.
- Red appears exactly once per view — the single loud voice.
- tabbed-gallery: left rail of oversized numerals switches pages; asymmetric card grid.

## Fit

Best for: design reviews, manifesto-grade readouts, portfolio-adjacent decks.
Avoid for: warm workshops, long comfortable reading sessions.
