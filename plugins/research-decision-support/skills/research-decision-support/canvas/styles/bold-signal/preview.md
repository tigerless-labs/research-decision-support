## Typography

Ultra-black display (Archivo Black stack) over Space Grotesk/system body at 15px.

## Signature moves

- One promoted full-saturation block per view, dark ink on it, poster-scale title.
- Oversized index numerals top-left; small-caps breadcrumbs with opacity states top-right.
- tabbed-gallery: breadcrumbs switch pages; first card promoted to the accent block.

## Fit

Best for: pitch moments, kickoff energy, one-message-per-screen readouts.
Avoid for: dense evidence tables, quiet institutional documents.
