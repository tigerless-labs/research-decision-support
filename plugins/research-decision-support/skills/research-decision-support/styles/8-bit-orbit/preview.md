## Typography

Monospace uppercase throughout (13px); wide-tracked headings, glow on titles only.

## Signature moves

- 3px accent borders with hard offset shadows — sprite-art panels, no blur anywhere.
- Level/world numbering motif; blinking cursor as the single animation.
- tabbed-gallery: world-select buttons switch pages; cards are cartridge tiles in a grid.

## Fit

Best for: hackathon demos, launch teasers, dev-community showpieces.
Avoid for: formal decision readouts, print-bound documents.
