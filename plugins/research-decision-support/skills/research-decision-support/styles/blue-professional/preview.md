## Typography

## Signature moves

- Warm cream on every surface; 4% cobalt tint cards with translucent cobalt hairlines.
- Single-accent discipline: cobalt carries every emphasis; grays subordinate.
- tabbed-gallery: pill tabs (active fills solid cobalt); eyebrow-led tinted cards.

## Fit

Best for: consulting deliverables, investor readouts, research synthesis for executives.
Avoid for: playful workshops, contexts that need heat or multiple accents.
