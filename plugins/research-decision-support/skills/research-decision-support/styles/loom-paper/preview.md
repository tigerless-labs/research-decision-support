## Typography

System UI stack, 14px base, 1.5 line-height; bold headings, no display faces.

## Signature moves

- Hairline grid borders everywhere; no shadows, no gradients.
- One accent hue per research layer, used only on chips, pills, and left borders.
- Single capped reading measure; chrome recedes to muted gray.

## Fit

Best for: default workbench projections, long reading sessions, mixed audiences.
Avoid for: high-energy pitch moments, dense single-screen dashboards.
