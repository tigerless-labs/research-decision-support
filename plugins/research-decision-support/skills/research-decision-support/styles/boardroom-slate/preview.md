## Typography

Serif headlines (Georgia stack) over 14px system sans body; small-caps status labels.

## Signature moves

- White hairline-edged cards on cool gray; zero shadows, zero decoration.
- accent-c carries the deepest hue: indigo rules and small-caps labels lead each entry.
- Print-adjacent: numbered sections, static state, survives PDF export.

## Fit

Best for: stakeholder decision readouts, ADR presentations, documents headed for print.
Avoid for: hackathon demos, playful workshops.
