## Typography

System sans at 15px with 1.65 line-height; heavy (800) friendly headings.

## Signature moves

- Big rounded cards (14px radius) on cream, one idea per card, generous air.
- Layer hues as a four-pen set: fat left borders + matching lowercase pills.
- Amber ideas layer sets the temperature — a thinking-out-loud style.

## Fit

Best for: workshops, idea-layer walkthroughs, brainstorm shareouts.
Avoid for: formal stakeholder readouts, dense evidence tables.
