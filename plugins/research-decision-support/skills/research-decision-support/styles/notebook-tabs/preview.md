## Typography

Serif display (Bodoni/Didot stack) over 14px system sans; ruled lines under section titles.

## Signature moves

- One elevated paper sheet on a dark desk; dotted-rule entries instead of boxed cards.
- Pastel index tabs on the sheet edge do the wayfinding; binder holes as the only ornament.
- tabbed-gallery: vertical edge tabs switch pages; active tab protrudes at full strength.

## Fit

Best for: editorial shareouts, reading-heavy reviews, warm stakeholder sessions.
Avoid for: dense dashboards, hard-edged engineering readouts.
