## Typography

Monospace body (13px, 1.45), sans headings at 600; numerics column-aligned.

## Signature moves

- Square corners, thin ledger rules, tight 16px rhythm — density without shrinking ink.
- Layer hues as syntax highlighting: bracketed uppercase status tags.
- Tabular layouts first; wide 1320px measure to hold evidence tables.

## Fit

Best for: engineering reviews, dense evidence tables, screen-share deep dives.
Avoid for: warm community shareouts, print handouts.
