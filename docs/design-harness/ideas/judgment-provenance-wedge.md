# The differentiating wedge: make "my judgment" a first-class, traceable object that leads to design

The four competitor clusters (see the competitors group in the
[sources index](../sources/index.md)) stop at discovery, extraction, screening, and storage
respectively; none lets a judgment carry its provenance and flow into the design layer. This
project does not compete upstream (the output of Zotero / discovery graphs can feed straight
into the sources layer); it does only the second half they all lack: judgment -> idea -> design
-> decision, traceable end to end. This is also the source of its generality -- any "read
material, make a decision" scenario (technology selection, due diligence, competitive analysis)
applies, not just academia.
