# Cards touched in the latest round carry a marker — the next round takes it over

The human's judgment (2026-07-14): cards changed in the most recent round of edits get a special
visual marker on the canvas; when the next round of changes lands, the marking refreshes — the
old round's markers clear and only the new round's cards are highlighted. Exactly one round is
ever marked. The marker is a derived projection (from the [ledger](../../logs.md)), never a field
stored on the card.
