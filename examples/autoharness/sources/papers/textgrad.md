# [论文] TextGrad: Automatic "Differentiation" via Text

**Yuksekgonul, Bianchi, Boen, Liu, Huang, Guestrin, Zou (Stanford)** — [arXiv:2406.07496](https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.07496) · 2024.

Backpropagates **natural-language "gradients"** (textual feedback) through a compound LLM system to optimize its components.

**Relevance to autoharness:** makes the "gradient descent in symbol space" analogy concrete — textual feedback as the backward signal, the mechanism SkillOpt later operationalizes with a held-out gate.
