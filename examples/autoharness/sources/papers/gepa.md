# [论文] GEPA: Reflective Prompt Evolution Can Outperform Reinforcement Learning

**(authors not captured)** — [arXiv:2507.19457](https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.19457) · 2025.

Shows **reflective prompt evolution** (genetic-Pareto search over prompts guided by reflection) can beat RL fine-tuning at far lower sample cost.

**Relevance to autoharness:** the strongest recent evidence that symbol-space optimization rivals weight-space — and the Pareto-reflective search pattern echoed by [EvoSkill](evoskill.md).
