**(authors not captured)** — [arXiv:2606.04536](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.04536) · 2026-06 · no public code.

Distills supervision into fast **LoRA weights online** — parametric memory rather than a text artifact.

**Relevance to autoharness:** the **counter-example** to the weight-free thesis. Where the symbolic-learning line learns the *document*, TMEM learns the *weights* — useful as the contrast that sharpens autoharness's "learn the symbol layer, stay model-portable" stance.
